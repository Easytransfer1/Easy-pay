Easy Transfer Refund Policy

Last Updated: 18 november 2024

At Easy Transfer, we strive to provide the best possible service to our customers. However, we understand that sometimes, things don't go as planned. That's why we have a refund policy in place to ensure that our customers are protected.

Scope of Refund Policy

This refund policy applies to all transactions made through Easy Transfer, including but not limited to:

  • Money transfers
  • Payment processing
  • Other services offered by Easy Transfer

Eligibility for Refund

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following conditions:

  • You must have made a transaction through Easy Transfer.
  • You must have experienced an issue with the transaction, such as a delay or failure.
  • You must have notified us of the issue within a reasonable timeframe.

Reasons for Refund

We understand that sometimes, things don't go as planned. Here are some reasons why you may be eligible for a refund:

  • Transaction was delayed or failed.
  • Transaction was processed incorrectly.
  • Recipient did not receive the funds.
  • Sender's account was debited incorrectly.
  • Other issues related to the transaction.

Refund Process

To initiate the refund process, please follow these steps:

  1. Login to your Easy Transfer account.
  2. Go to the "Transaction History" page.
  3. Find the transaction you want to refund and click on it.
  4. Click on the "Refund" button and follow the prompts.
  5. Provide a detailed explanation of the reason for the refund request.
  6. Submit the refund request.

Once we receive your refund request, we will review it and respond within 3-5 business days.

Refund Timeline

Please note that the refund process may take up to 10 business days to complete, depending on the payment method and processing time.

Refund Amount

The refund amount will be the full amount of the original transaction, minus any applicable fees.

Disclaimer

Easy Transfer reserves the right to modify or update this refund policy at any time without prior notice.
